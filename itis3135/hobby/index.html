How It Works

Drifting requires a perfect balance of throttle, steering, and brake control. The driver needs to initiate the drift by steering the car into a corner while applying the throttle, causing the rear tires to lose traction.

Mastering drifting takes practice, coordination, and understanding of car dynamics.

Why I Love Drifting
