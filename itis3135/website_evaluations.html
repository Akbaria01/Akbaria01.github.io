Website Evaluations

Craigslist

Craigslist is a highly popular classified ads website known for its minimalistic and outdated design.

  • Contrast: Poor contrast with a dull gray color scheme and lack of visual emphasis.
  • Repetition: Repetitive in its simplicity, but lacks any creativity or diversity in layout.
  • Alignment: The alignment of elements feels cluttered and random with too many text links.
  • Proximity: Links and elements are packed closely together, making it hard to focus on key information.

Khan Academy

Khan Academy is an educational platform offering free courses and resources, with a clean and engaging user interface.

  • Contrast: Excellent use of contrast between text and background, making content easy to read and navigate.
  • Repetition: Consistent branding across all pages with repetition of fonts, colors, and layout for a cohesive experience.
  • Alignment: Well-aligned elements with clear headings, subheadings, and logical content structure for ease of use.
  • Proximity: Content is grouped effectively, making it easy to follow and navigate through different educational resources.
